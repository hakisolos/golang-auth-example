# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect everyone who participates in this project to uphold these standards.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful** - Treat everyone with respect regardless of their experience level, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.
- **Be Inclusive** - We welcome and support people of all backgrounds.
- **Be Collaborative** - Work together effectively and constructively.
- **Be Constructive** - Provide feedback that helps others improve.
- **Focus on What is Best** - We strive to make the community and project better.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment in any form is not tolerated:
- Offensive comments related to any of the characteristics mentioned above
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Harassment through multiple channels
- Sexual comments or imagery
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Discrimination or derogatory language
